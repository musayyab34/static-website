About

Learn about our history.

  • 2009-2011

    Laying the Foundation

    CloudWave began as a small team of cloud enthusiasts with a vision to make cloud technology accessible to businesses of all sizes. These early years were dedicated to research and development, laying the groundwork for what would become a leading cloud solutions provider.

  • March 2011

    Entering the Market

    With our first official product launch, CloudWave entered the market, offering cloud integration services that quickly gained traction. Our innovative approach attracted early adopters and set the stage for rapid growth.

  • December 2015

    Expanding Our Horizons

    As the demand for cloud services grew, so did CloudWave. We expanded our offerings to include comprehensive cloud solutions, from data migration to security enhancement, serving a diverse range of industries.

  • July 2020

    Scaling New Heights

    In response to the global shift towards remote work and digital transformation, CloudWave scaled its operations, launching new products designed to support businesses in navigating the complexities of cloud adoption.
